Weather Prediction App

ABOUT

This is a simple machine learning app which predicts the current day temperature and rain based on the weather conditions on the past three days. We have made use of kaggle dataset for makeing this app and have managed to achieve 90% average accuracy for this app. This Project is submmited by: 1. Kasaragadda Vinod 2.
